Sources:
- Hexagon Kit (https://kenney.nl/assets/hexagon-kit)
  the whole battlefield board — grass/dirt/water/stone/hill hex tiles, the
  enemy gatehouse and your castle — tessellated and rendered to battlefield.png.
- Castle Kit (https://kenney.nl/assets/castle-kit)
  stone build plots, the catapult and hexagon frost (trebuchet) towers, and
  several enemies — rendered from the kit's GLB models to isometric PNGs.
- Mini Forest (https://kenney.nl/assets/mini-forest)
  the archer tower, plus tents/trees/rocks/plants scattered across the board.
- Survival Kit (https://kenney.nl/assets/survival-kit)
  battlefield scenery — tents, campfires, logs, barrels, crates, workbenches,
  rocks and trees.
- Platformer Kit (https://kenney.nl/assets/platformer-kit)
  battlefield scenery — flowers, mushrooms, plants, grass tufts, hedges, crates.
- Mini Arena (https://kenney.nl/assets/mini-arena)
  soldier enemy — rendered from GLB.
- Mini Dungeon (https://kenney.nl/assets/mini-dungeon)
  orc enemy and the paved-path floor tiles (recoloured) — rendered from GLB.
- Graveyard Kit (https://kenney.nl/assets/graveyard-kit)
  skeleton and vampire enemies — rendered from GLB.
- Nature Kit (https://kenney.nl/assets/nature-kit)
  trees, stones, bushes, flowers, mushrooms, grass — the kit's own
  isometric renders.
- Tower Defense (top-down) (https://kenney.nl/assets/tower-defense-top-down)
  projectile sprites (shell, spark, rocket) and coin.
